Making Tea

1.Boil water in a pot

2.Add tea leaves and sugar

3.Let it simmer for a few minutes

4.Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired

Making Paratha and Aloo curry

1.Knead the dough for paratha

2.Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee

3.For aloo curry ,saute onions,tomato and spices in a pan

4.Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickness

Making Briyani

1.Marinate the meat and vegetables with spices and yougurt

2.Cook basmati rice until its 70% done

3.Layer the marinated meat/vegetables and rice in pot

4.Cook on low heat(dum) until fully cooked
